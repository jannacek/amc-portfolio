Art & Technology Lab · Community · Sensor-Based Installation

AT-LAB I

A 3-day participatory art and technology lab in the province of Groningen — a custom-built immersive space where ~50 community participants shaped sound, light, and visuals through body movement and heartbeat sensors. Part of the NWO-funded HLaS research project.

Community Sensor Technology Sound & Light HLaS / NWO 2023

Field Research · Landscape · Iceland

P.E.L.E.

A field research expedition across Iceland's volcanic landscape — from lava tubes at Lofthellir to the geothermal shores of Mývatn and the ancient rifts of Þingvellir. Named after the goddess of volcanoes, the project investigates primordial geological forces as data systems.

Volcanic Landscape Field Research Iceland Geological Data 2023

Spatial Audio · 4DSOUND · Residency · London

Stone Nest

A 5-day residential course at Stone Nest, Soho, working within a bespoke 4DSOUND system — exploring spatial audio as a creative medium. Organised by Amoenus in collaboration with the Amsterdam-based 4DSOUND studio, culminating in a public closing concert.

4DSOUND Spatial Audio Residency London 2023

Sound · Data · Performance

Data Resonance

A practice-based research project exploring data surveillance, data dignity, and the emotionality of data through psychoacoustics and performative sonification. Motivated by the societal implications of the Covid-19 pandemic and the 1.5-metre society.

Psychoacoustics Sonification Data Dignity KIEM 2020 2020 – 2021

AI · Projection Mapping · Visual Storytelling · Festival

Luna

Des-illu(minat)/s-ion(s) — a site-specific spatial projection commissioned by Media Art Friesland for LUNA Festival 2019 in Leeuwarden. Historical data about the Blokhuispoort was processed through machine learning to generate an AI-authored narrative, translated back into a moving image projected on the building's walls.

AI / Machine Learning Projection Mapping Leeuwarden Arvustin Caramdersson 2019
